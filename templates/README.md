# Email Templates for AI Coaching

These templates are for David's use in client communications. Customize as needed for each situation.

## Templates Included

1. **booking-confirmation.md** - Sent after someone books a strategy call
2. **pre-call-intake.md** - Questions to send before the call
3. **post-call-followup.md** - Summary and next steps after the call
4. **outreach-linkedin.md** - LinkedIn connection/message template
5. **outreach-email-cold.md** - Cold email to prospects
6. **outreach-email-warm.md** - Warm introduction via referral
